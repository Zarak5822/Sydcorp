What's the difference
between Level 1 ASP &
Level 2 ASP Electricians?
Level 1 electricians are permitted to connect residential and commercial properties to the electricity network. They can extend network capacity as well as perform electrical installations, repairs and maintenance to residential and commercial properties.

A Level 2 ASP has advanced training and certification, allowing them to work on live electrical services, whether they are overhead or underground. Working on the electrical supply network requires advanced knowledge of safety as well as specialist knowledge due to the danger and complexity of the work involved.
You would need a level 2 electrician if you are planning to:
  • Connect your home, business or any other property
    to the main electricity distribution network.
  • Have a solar net metering
  • Provide temporary power supply for builders
Everything is covered from upgrading your existing electricity supply to major electrical work.

Q:Do I have to stop using my shower? and when can I start using it again after the work has been completed?
A:If the shower is leaking and causing damage then yes, it is preferable to stop using the shower. Once the shower has been re-grouted it will be 24 hours before it can be reused.
Q:Should I have my shower screen replaced before or after the re-grouting?
A:If you are considering having the shower screen replaced it is recommended to have this done before the re-grouting work.
Q:How long does the work take?
A:For standard shower re-grout, I would allow between 3-4 hours but it can vary depending on how hard the grout is to remove, size of the tiles, width of grout joints etc.
